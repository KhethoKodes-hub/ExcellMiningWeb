Our Story

Engineering Excellence in Mining

Building partnerships, delivering solutions, and empowering communities across South Africa's mining sector

In 2015, Gladwin Rainers, a certified boilermaker, established a small mining services firm in Kathu, Northern Cape, near Kumba Iron Ore's Sishen Mine. With a vision to create sustainable job opportunities and foster economic growth in the community, Excell Mining began its journey.

Today, Excell Mining employs around 72 individuals and aspires to become the foremost mining services provider in the region. The company specialises in services like bucket refurbishment, maintenance, straps, and steel fabrication.

Rapid growth over the last three years has seen its workforce grow from 25 in 2019 to its current size. Most employees are locals, including skilled artisans, administrative staff, and safety personnel.

Visionary Leadership

Gladwin Rainers

CEO & Managing Director | Certified Red Seal Boilermaker

Founder of Excell Mining

Drawing inspiration from his father's enduring legacy, Gladwin Rainers founded Excell Mining with a powerful vision: to combat high unemployment rates in Kathu by creating sustainable job opportunities and fostering economic growth. As a Certified Red Seal Boilermaker, Gladwin brings technical excellence and hands-on expertise to every project.

His leadership philosophy centers on empowering communities through skill development and meaningful employment, building not just a successful company, but a brighter future for the Northern Cape region. Under his guidance, Excell Mining has become synonymous with quality, innovation, and social responsibility in the mining services sector.

Our Journey

Company Timeline

Key milestones in Excell Mining's growth and development

2015

Company Foundation

Gladwin Rainers establishes Excell Mining as a small mining services firm in Kathu, Northern Cape, near Kumba Iron Ore's Sishen Mine.

2015

Dingleton Resettlement Project

Excell contributes to the Dingleton resettlement project, delivering steelworks as a sub-contractor to the main contractor.

2019

Significant Growth

Workforce grows from 25 employees to current size, with most employees being locals from the Kathu community.

2021

Service Expansion

Company expands service offerings to include crane hire, civil maintenance, and diversified fabrication capabilities.

2023

Current Operations

Excell Mining now employs 72 individuals and serves as a critical supplier for major mining operations in the region.
