Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and operations

What areas do you serve?

+

We primarily serve the Northern Cape region, with a focus on mining operations in and around Kathu, including Sishen Mine, Kolomela Mine, and surrounding areas. We also undertake projects throughout South Africa.

How quickly can you respond to emergency maintenance requests?

+

For emergency maintenance requests, we aim to respond within 2-4 hours during business hours. We understand that downtime costs money in mining operations, so we prioritize urgent requests to minimize disruption to your operations.

Do you provide 24/7 services?

+

While our standard business hours are Monday to Friday, 7:00 AM to 5:00 PM, we do offer emergency services for critical situations. Please call our emergency line for after-hours support, which may involve additional charges.

What is your typical response time for quotation requests?

+

We typically respond to quotation requests within 1-2 business days. For complex projects requiring detailed assessment, it may take up to 5 business days to provide a comprehensive quote.

Are you able to work on-site at mining operations?

+

Yes, our team is fully equipped and trained to work on-site at mining operations. All our personnel have the necessary safety certifications and we comply with all mine safety standards and protocols.
