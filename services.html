GET Maintenance and Equipment Rebuilds

Ensuring machinery operates at optimal efficiency

Our GET (Ground Engaging Tools) maintenance service focuses on maintaining and rebuilding the critical components that directly interact with the ground during mining operations. Proper maintenance of these tools significantly influences productivity, fuel efficiency, and equipment longevity.

Our GET Maintenance Services Include:

Bucket Refurbishment

Complete overhaul and reinforcement of mining buckets

Wear Parts Replacement

Replacement of teeth, edges, and other wear components

Component Rebuilding

Rebuilding of worn components to extend service life

Performance Optimization

Enhancements to improve digging efficiency and reduce fuel consumption
