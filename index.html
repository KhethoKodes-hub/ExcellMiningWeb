Company Profile

Engineering Excellence in Mining

Building partnerships, delivering solutions, and empowering communities across South Africa's mining sector

Our Foundation

Established in 2015 by certified boilermaker Gladwin Rainers in Kathu, Northern Cape, near Kumba Iron Ore's Sishen Mine. From humble beginnings, we've grown into a workforce of 72 dedicated professionals, driven by our commitment to excellence and community empowerment.

Our Growth

Experiencing remarkable expansion from 25 employees in 2019 to our current team size, we primarily employ local talent including skilled artisans, administrative staff, and safety personnel. Our growth reflects our dedication to creating sustainable employment opportunities in the Northern Cape.

Our Impact

Major contributor to the Dingleton resettlement project (2015), delivering complex steelworks. We continue to serve as a critical supplier of ground-engaging tools and maintenance services to Sishen Mine, with extensive experience in mechanical and piping projects across the region.

Leadership

Visionary Leadership

Gladwin Rainers

CEO & Managing Director | Certified Red Seal Boilermaker

Drawing inspiration from his father's enduring legacy, Gladwin Rainers founded Excell Mining with a powerful vision: to combat high unemployment rates in Kathu by creating sustainable job opportunities and fostering economic growth. As a Certified Red Seal Boilermaker, Gladwin brings technical excellence and hands-on expertise to every project. His leadership philosophy centers on empowering communities through skill development and meaningful employment, building not just a successful company, but a brighter future for the Northern Cape region. Under his guidance, Excell Mining has become synonymous with quality, innovation, and social responsibility in the mining services sector.
