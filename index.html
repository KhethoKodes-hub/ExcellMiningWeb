Company Profile

Our Guiding Principle: "Faith meets opportunity meets success."

In 2015, Gladwin Rainers, a certified boilermaker, established a small mining services firm in Kathu, Northern Cape, near Kumba Iron Ore's Sishen Mine. Today, Excell Mining employs around 72 individuals and aspires to become the foremost mining services provider in the region.

The company specialises in services like bucket refurbishment, maintenance, straps, and steel fabrication. Rapid growth over the last three years has seen its workforce grow from 25 in 2019 to its current size. Most employees are locals, including skilled artisans, administrative staff, and safety personnel.

Excell contributed to the Dingleton resettlement project in 2015, delivering steelworks as a sub-contractor to the main contractor. The company has also undertaken numerous mechanical and piping projects for Sishen and serves as a critical supplier of ground-engaging tools and maintenance services.

Leadership

Gladwin Rainers

CEO and Managing Director | Certified Red Seal Boilermaker

Gladwin Rainers, the CEO and Managing Director of Excell, is a Certified Red Seal Boilermaker who draws inspiration from his father's enduring legacy. Witnessing the challenges of high unemployment rates in Kathu, Gladwin was driven to make a meaningful impact by establishing Excell. His vision centers on creating sustainable job opportunities and fostering economic growth within the community. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, Gladwin's leadership reflects his passion for empowering others, contributing to a brighter future for Kathu through skill development and employment initiatives.
